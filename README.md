## What BTC War Does

BTC War is a web-based crypto visualization app centered on Bitcoin market activity. It brings together live BTC pricing, BTC/USDT order flow, market depth, liquidity, and executed trades in one browser view so the market can be scanned quickly and explored visually.

Built with WebGL and Three.js, it presents market motion as an interactive 3D battlefield. The result is a more visual way to follow trading pressure, volume patterns, and market structure without leaving the browser.
